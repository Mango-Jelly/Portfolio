    TCP/IP — Transport Layer Implementation

    An end-to-end IP forwarding engine in Go with a from-scratch RIP routing protocol (longest-prefix match, split horizon, poison reverse) and a user-space TCP stack featuring the full handshake/teardown state machine, sliding-window flow control and retransmission — transmitting 2 Mb in 0.2s. Extended into a functioning encrypted VPN tunnel.
